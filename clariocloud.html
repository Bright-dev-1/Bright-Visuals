Social Media Designs

Clario Cloud.

Elevating the digital presence of a next-generation web hosting platform.

Client

Clario Cloud

The Challenge

Clario Cloud is a rapidly growing web hosting service offering ultra-fast, secure, and scalable servers for startups and established enterprises. Despite having a superior technical product, their social media presence lacked the modern, tech-forward aesthetic required to build trust in a highly competitive market.

Bright Visuals was tasked with completely overhauling their social media design strategy. The goal was to create a cohesive, visually striking feed that communicates reliability, speed, and cutting-edge technology while driving engagement and server sales.

The Solution

We developed a dark-mode inspired visual identity for their social channels, utilizing deep neon blues and electric purples to signify "the cloud" and "speed." By combining sleek typography, 3D abstract cloud elements, and highly legible infographic styles, we transformed complex hosting plans into easily digestible, highly engaging social media creatives.
